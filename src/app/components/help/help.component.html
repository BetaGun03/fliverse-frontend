Help & User Guide

What is Fliverse?

Fliverse is your personal universe to discover, search, and explore information about movies and series. You can add your own content, manage personal lists, track what you have watched, and connect with a community of movie and series lovers.

Getting Started

  1. Register or Login: Use your Google account to register or log in. You can also use the classic registration form.
  2. Explore the Catalog: Browse the home page to discover trending or recently added movies and series.
  3. Search: Use the search bar or advanced search to find content by title, genre, type, or keywords.

Main Features

  • Content Details: Click on any movie or series to view detailed information, including synopsis, genres, year, duration, and trailer.
  • Watched Status: Mark content as "Watched" or "To Watch" to keep track of your viewing history.
  • Personal Lists: Create and manage your own lists to organize your favorite movies and series.
  • Add Content: Contribute by adding new movies or series to the platform for everyone to discover.
  • User Profile: Access your profile to view and edit your personal information, change your password, or upload a profile picture.
  • Logout: Log out from your current device or from all devices for security.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password? You can change your password from your user profile. If you forget your password, use Google login or contact support.
  • How is my data protected? Fliverse uses Google Auth for secure login and follows GDPR-compliant privacy practices. See the Privacy Policy for details.
  • How can I delete my account? Contact support at support@fliverse.es to request account deletion.
