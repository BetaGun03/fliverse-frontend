Privacy Policy

Executive Summary

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and what rights users have regarding their information. Fliverse (https://fliverse.es) is a web application that allows you to view, search for, and contribute information about movies and series. To provide a secure and reliable service, we use Google Auth Platform as our login provider. We comply with GDPR and other applicable data protection regulations.

1. Data Controller

Entity: Fliverse
Contact Email: support@fliverse.es

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data Provided by the User

Name, surname, and email address: obtained via Google Auth Platform when you register or log in.
Uploaded content: titles, synopses, dates, images, or links for movies and series you contribute.

2.2 Usage and Technical Data

Access logs: date and time of your last visit, partially anonymized IP, browser, and device.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • Authentication and account management: verify your identity and manage your profile via Google Auth Platform.
  • Service provision: display the catalog, enable searches, and allow content uploads.
  • Continuous improvement: aggregated statistical analysis to optimize features and personalize recommendations.
  • Communication: send important notifications about updates, incidents, or changes to this Privacy Policy.

4. Legal Basis

  • Performance of contract/service: authentication and basic use of the application.
  • Consent: for marketing or commercial communications (if you opt in).
  • Legitimate interest: maintaining security, fraud prevention, and product improvement.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

  • Essential cookies: ensure proper authentication and platform functionality.
  • Performance cookies: collect anonymous data to analyze traffic (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functionality cookies: remember your preferences (language, theme).

How to configure: manage or reject cookies via your browser settings or by clicking “Cookie Preferences” in the footer.

6. Data Sharing and Transfers

  • Service providers: Google (authentication), hosting, and analytics platforms (under data protection agreements).
  • No third-party sharing without consent: we do not share your data with unrelated third parties except when legally required.
  • International transfers: Google Auth Platform may process data outside the EU under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

7. Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: retained while the account is active or until deletion request.
  • Contributed content: until you request its removal; may remain anonymized for statistical purposes.
  • Logs and metrics: retained for up to 12 months before anonymization or deletion.

8. Users’ Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, send a request to support@fliverse.es. We will respond within one month.

9. Security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all communications.
  • Internal access controls and data protection training.
  • Regular vulnerability assessments and backups.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Policy. We will notify you in advance via a website notice or email. The current version will always be available in the application footer.

11. Contact & Complaints

If you have questions or wish to file a complaint:
Email: support@fliverse.es
Website: https://fliverse.es
You may also contact the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) if you believe your rights have not been properly addressed.
